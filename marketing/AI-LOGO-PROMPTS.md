## üìã STYLE GUIDE

**Brand Colors:**
- Primary: `#9945FF` (Solana Purple)
- Secondary: `#14F195` (Solana Green)
- Accent: `#00D4AA` (Memory Teal)
- Background: `#1a1a2e` (Dark Navy)
- Text: `#ffffff` (White)

**Typography (for reference):**
- Headings: Inter Bold / SF Pro Bold
- Body: Inter Regular / SF Pro Regular
- Mono: JetBrains Mono (code snippets)

**Logo Usage Rules:**
- Min size: 32px height
- Clear space: 20% of logo height on all sides
- Don't: Rotate, skew, outline, add effects
- Do: Use on dark OR light backgrounds (provide variants)
